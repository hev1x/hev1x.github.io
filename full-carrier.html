Service Package Full Carrier Package

Overview

The Full Carrier Package is designed for carrier companies with company drivers who require comprehensive management of accidents and insurance claims.

This package is ideal for medium to large-sized carriers that need to ensure smooth operations without the burden of handling claims internally.


Procedure

  1. Accident Reporting: Immediate notification to TAOClaim upon an incident.
  2. Data Collection: Gathering all relevant accident details and documentation.
  3. Coordination: Quick coordination with the carrier's safety team.
  4. Claims Handling: Complete management of the insurance claim process.
  5. Follow-Up: Regular updates and follow-ups until the claim is resolved.

Benefits

  • Efficient Management: Streamlined processes to handle accidents swiftly.
  • Operational Continuity: Minimizes disruptions to the carrier's operations.
  • Expert Support: Professional advice for drivers and safety officers.
  • Comprehensive Reporting: Detailed accident analysis and report generation.

Operations

  • Daily Assistance: Help in avoiding cargo claims while the driver is at the consignee.
  • Damage Reimbursement: Handling reimbursements for any equipment damages.
  • Accident Analysis: Thorough investigation and analysis to prevent future occurrences.

Step-by-Step Process

  1. Notify TAOClaim: Report the accident immediately.
  2. Collect Information: Gather all necessary details, including driver statements and photographic evidence.
  3. Coordinate: TAOClaim works with your safety team to handle the claim submission.
  4. Manage Claims: We oversee the entire claims process, keeping you updated.
  5. Finalize Resolution: Ensure all reimbursements and compensations are handled appropriately.

We are aviable 24/7 for any questions regarding a existing claim or a potential claim.
